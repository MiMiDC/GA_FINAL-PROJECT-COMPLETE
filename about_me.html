About Robert Gately

Robert Gately was born on Miami Beach, the second of five children born to Bostonian Immigrants to South Florida. His idyllic childhood was marred only by school. His parents were known for their cocktail parties and Robert was impressed into servitude at an early age in the kitchen assisting in the creation of all sorts of Hors d'oeuvre and petit fours.

As an adult his varied jobs have included a stint in the United States Air Force, drapery installer, computer technician for a large aerospace company, sales manager in an office supply store, and technical support representative for an Electronic Medical Record software company. He lives in Seattle with his super smart wife and two rescued terrier mix dogs, Baxter and Dylan.
