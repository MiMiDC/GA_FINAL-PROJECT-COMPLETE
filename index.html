Healthy Balsamic Vinaigrette


  • tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp NuSalt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper, ground
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil

Directions:

  1. In blender, mix all ingredients except oil together until well combined.
  2. Set blender to lowest speed and slowly add oil to the mixture until emulsified.
Servings: 6

Tips & Tricks

CHOOSE FRESH FOODS instead of packaged options. Fresh cuts of meats contain a small amount of naturally occuring sodium, but it is far less than the amount used in processed meats and pre-packaged foods. If a food item keeps well in the fridge for days or weeks, it's a good indication that it has a high sodium content.

Tips & Tricks

CHECK NUTRITION LABELS: Understanding how to read the Nutrition Facts label on food packages is important. Comparing labels is a great tool to help you make healthy food choices that you’ll be able to use moving forward. (Courtesy of fda.gov)
